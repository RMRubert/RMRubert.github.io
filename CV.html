Ricardo Montero Rubert

Hydraulic Engineer with interest in Data Analysis

Ricardo is a Civil Engineer (Meng in Civil Engineering) who specialises in computational hydraulics (MSc in Hydro-Informatics and Water Management).

Within his current job, Ricardo works as a free-flow hydraulic modeler, predominantly working on flood risk assessments and river navigation.

He has also worked across different disciplines, developing flood forecasting tools, working as a GIS/CAD specialist and as a Quality/Benchmarking assistant whenever it was required. Whilst he was studying, he worked as a data analyst developer at the CREALP institute (Switzerland), and as project management assistant at ACIEROID (France)
